In

 the

  desert

   I

    saw

     a

      creature,

       naked,

        bestial,

         Who,

          squatting

           upon

            the

            ground,

             held

              his

               heart

                in

                 his

                  hands,

                   And

                    ate

                     of

                      it.

                       I

                        said,

                         Is

                          it

                           good

                            friend?

                             It

                              is

                               bitter

                                bitter,

                                 he

                                   answered;

                                   But

                                    I

                                     like

                                      it

                                       Because

                                        it

                                         is

                                          bitter,

                                           And

                                            because

                                             it

                                              is

                                               my

                                                heart.
