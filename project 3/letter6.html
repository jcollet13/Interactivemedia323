Dear Hailey,

Not one day has passed that I haven't had the urge to pick up the phone and call you just to hear your voice and to know that you're doing okay. My mind is flooded with thoughts and memories of the great moments we shared, and I will always cherish these memories.

Whilst I do not regret our relationship, I do lament the way in which it was ended over such distance; as I'm sure you are aware it is not by any means my favoured way of discussing important issues. To say that I am sure breaking up was the best thing for us to do now would be a blatent act of self-deception. I am not certain it was the right decision, nor am I certain it was the wrong decision. What I am certain of is that I love you more than I ever thought possible, and that you will always remain a very special person to me.

I consider myself so blessed to have been able to share so many great memories with such a beautiful, caring and fun young lady.

While I know you wished not to hear from me again, I felt it necessary from my mind's pandemonium to write you this letter.

I love you Hailey. You will always have a special place in my heart.

-AJ
